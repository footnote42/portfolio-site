## Feature: [Name]

### 1. Problem / Opportunity
What friction or gap does this address? Or: What am I curious about exploring?

**Real-world use case:**
[Describe actual scenario where this matters]

---

### 2. Alignment Check

**Does this fit with:**
- [ ] **Inspire vibe coders mission?** (Will this help someone else take the leap?)
- [ ] **Authentic journey narrative?** (Is this genuinely part of my learning, or trying to look impressive?)
- [ ] **Current learning goals?** (What am I trying to understand right now?)

**Exploration & Challenge:**
- [ ] **Am I exploring an interesting idea?** (Even if it doesn't "solve a problem")
- [ ] **Does this challenge convention or typical AI design?** (Or is it playing it safe?)
- [ ] **Will this be enjoyable to build?** (If it feels like grinding, why am I doing it?)

**If alignment is weak:** What's the actual reason I want to build this? Be honest.

---

### 7. Reflection Prompts (Post-Build)

Once shipped, consider:
- What surprised me?
- What would I do differently?
- What did I learn that I didn't expect to learn?
- Is this actually aligned with my goals, or did I get distracted?

*These don't need answers upfront - they're for after you've built it.*
